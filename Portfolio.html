About Me

Hello, everyone! My name is Brad, I am a USAF Veteran, currently working as an Assistant Language Teacher (ALT) in
Yamaguchi, Japan. During my time in the Air Force, I spent one year each in Louisiana and South Korea, and a little over
three years in Italy. After I left the military, I moved to Japan in May of 2017 in order to begin my undergraduate degree at
Temple University, Japan Campus in Tokyo. In December 2020, I graduated with a BA in Asian Studies, focusing on Gender
Inequality in Japan. Since graduation, I have moved to Yamaguchi where I teach English to elementary and preschool aged children.
